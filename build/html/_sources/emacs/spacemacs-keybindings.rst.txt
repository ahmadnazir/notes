Spacemacs Keybindings
=====================

`SPC t m c` toggle mode-line org-clock

`SPC v v` expand
`SPC v V` contract

`SPC f t` neo tree

Custom
------

`SPC o o n` new entry in the journal (before I clock in)
`SPC o o i` Clock in (for the selected note)
`SPC o o o` Clock out
`SPC o o r` Generate the report
